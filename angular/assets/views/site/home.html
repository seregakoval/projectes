how it works

Mission statement

CourseRooms seeks to advance the field of e-learning by offering world-class, professional career focused course content from vetted tutors and institutions worldwide.

charity

One of the most powerful tools for empowering individuals is making sure that any individual who wants to receive a quality education can do so.

The Right to Education

Every child should have the opportunity to receive a quality education.

At CourseRooms, we strongly believe that each and every child should have the access and means to gaining a basic education, for this right - we have launched an initiative aimed at providing children basic schooling - solidifying their futures.

We also strongly believe and act upon the right of education for children regardless of their race, ethnicity and background. Education must not be limited to those from financially prosperous families, which is why we have introduced a program for underprivileged children - allowing them to comfortably and gratuitously attain this right.

The 1-1 Educational Program

At CourseRooms, we have introduced an educational instructors 1-1 program. For each course purchased another will be provided with a free course.

This initiative has been successfully launched in India with the aim to quickly roll this globally. Schooling must be made accessible to all children irrespective of their financial status or anything else for that matter. And we are doing everything we can to make this a reality.

“It is an honour and a privilege to be a part of the 1-1 program. Nothing gives me more joy to introduce this program in our prestigious educational institute - helping underprivileged children become positive and educated member of our society without having to burden them with anything. “

“We will do all we can to successfully spread this program all over the world. “

Vineet Garg: Vice-Management Commitee of A.S Khanna Colleges and Schools

“I was delighted to hear about this educational initiative. It is just what our great nation needs, unbiased and indiscriminate education for poor and underprivileged children who have great potential to become learned scholars and productive members of the society. “

“I look forward to starting this program in the near future.”

Kewal Dhillon: Vice President of SBBS Memorial Charitable Society
