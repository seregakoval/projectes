About Segmatic

Segmatic is the world’s most advanced ad copy generation tool

Search Made Simple

Segmatic gives you the ability to get as close to the character limit as possible, even with huge, complicated campaigns involving a mix of long and short words. You tell the platform your ad copy preferences - the logic you would ideally like to apply to ad copy, and what to do when the character limit has been hit or exceeded. Once Segmatic knows your preferred logic, and your second, third and fourth preferences, it will generate the right ad to the right person every time, at enormous scale.
