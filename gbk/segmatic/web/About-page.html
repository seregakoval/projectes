Our History

Dave Walker and Bart Storan founded Segmatic in 2014. Both worked previously worked as in-house marketers - Dave was Head of Conversion and Bidding for an international gaming company and Bart was Product Manager for an online payments company. Sharing frustrations with traditional approaches to paid search, Dave and Bart vowed to build the tech platform they wish they had when they were in-house marketers. What began as a Performance Marketing Agency, initially called Segmatic, managing Paid Search advertising for a range of clients, has entered a new phase with the development of the Segmatic Platform. Initially conceived as an in-house tool, we now realise that we need to share the platform with the world’s marketers.
