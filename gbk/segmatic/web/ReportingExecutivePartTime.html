Reporting Executive (Part Time)

About Segmatic:

Segmatic is a performance marketing agency & technology platform established in 2014 to bring the highest level of analytical vigour to Paid Search. The company’s directors have over 10 years’ experience with some of Ireland’s biggest eCommerce companies – such as Paddy Power – and now they are expanding their dedicated team to bring the lessons that they have learned in-house to other companies throughout Ireland and the UK. First and foremost we are a performance marketing company. We deliver results, plain and simple. In fact, we are so committed to this philosophy that that we bill based on performance. This is popular with our ever growing client base – which now includes T.M. Lewin, Betfair, Volusion, Prezzo to name but a few.

About the Team/Role:

This is a fast-paced and data-intensive position within the performance marketing team. The successful candidate will be working directly on Segmatic’s diverse client base to drive return on marketing investment.

They will be trained in using Excel to run daily reporting for all clients. This role will develop the Excel skills of the candidate significantly. This position would be ideal for a hard-working person who wants a part time role while maintaining flexibility. Previous experience in online is not required – but a basic numerical ability is a must.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Daily Excel Reporting: Collating Data from Multiple Sources,
  • Marketing Optimisation: Effective optimisation of online marketing campaigns driving significant increases in new customer acquisition.
  • Creativity: Utilisation of cutting edge auction marketing technology ensuring Segmatic is a market leader in the area.

Desired Skills and Experience

  • Highly numerate and strong logic skills.
  • Excel expertise a bonus.
  • Excellent Attention to Detail
  • Self Starter and quick learner. Confidence and ability to tackle new initiatives with little guidance
  • Demonstrated ability to perform numerical analysis – for example a minimum C1 in Higher Level Leaving Certificate Mathematics
